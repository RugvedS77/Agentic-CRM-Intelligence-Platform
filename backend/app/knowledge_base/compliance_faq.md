# Compliance FAQ

## GDPR and Data Privacy

GDPR deletion requests must be completed within 30 days.
Data access requests must be acknowledged within 72 hours.
Data portability requests (Article 20) must be fulfilled within 30 days.
Right to erasure requests require legal/compliance team review before action.
All GDPR-related emails must be flagged for legal review and must never receive an autonomous reply.

## Security Incidents

Security incidents must be escalated immediately to the Security Team.
Ransomware, breach, and data leak reports require immediate human intervention.
Never auto-reply to emails containing extortion, BTC payment demands, or threat of data exposure.

## Legal Requests

Legal requests require human review.
Cease and desist letters must be routed to the Legal Team.
Any mention of lawsuit, attorney, or legal action must trigger escalation.

## General Compliance

Compliance related emails must never receive autonomous approval.
All compliance actions must be logged in the audit trail.
Customer data requests must be verified against the CRM contact profile before any data is shared.
