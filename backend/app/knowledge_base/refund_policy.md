# Refund Policy

No refunds after 14 days.

## Exception Process

Refund requests submitted within 14 days of purchase are eligible for full refund.
Requests after 14 days require manager approval and are subject to a 25% administrative fee.
Enterprise contracts have custom terms defined in the signed agreement.

## Credits vs Refunds

Account credits are preferred for minor service issues.
Full refunds are reserved for service outages exceeding 24 hours.
Credits expire after 12 months and are non-transferable.

## Churn Retention Playbook

Customers threatening cancellation should be reviewed by Customer Success.
Customers threatening public reviews require escalation.
Offer credits before refunds where appropriate.
Enterprise customers should be routed for retention review.
Repeated complaints increase escalation priority.
